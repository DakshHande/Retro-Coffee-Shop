Refund Policy

Thank you for choosing **Retro Coffee Shop**! We value our customers, but please note that we have a **strict no-refund policy**. Please read carefully before placing an order.

❌ **All sales are final. We do not offer refunds under any circumstances.**

📌 **Please provide the correct and complete delivery address.** If you enter an incorrect or incomplete address:

📢 **You must report any issues within 30 minutes of delivery** with a clear photo of the received item.
