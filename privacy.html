Privacy Policy

Welcome to **Retro Coffee Shop**! Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. No Login or Sign-Up Required

🛑 We do **NOT** require you to **create an account, log in, or sign up**. You can place orders **directly via email** without providing unnecessary personal details.

2. What Information We Collect?

When you place an order via email, we collect:

  • 📌 **Your Name** (to identify the order)
  • 📌 **Your Contact Number** (to confirm the order)
  • 📌 **Your Delivery Address** (to deliver your order)
  • 📌 **Order Details** (for processing your request)

✅ **We DO NOT store sensitive payment details**. All payments are securely processed by **trusted payment gateways**.

3. How We Use Your Information?

  • 🚚 **To process and deliver your order**.
  • 📞 **To contact you for order confirmation or issues**.
  • 🔒 **We NEVER share or sell your information**.

4. Payment Security

💳 **All transactions are 100% secure**. We accept:

  • ✅ **Cash on Delivery (COD)**
  • ✅ **UPI, Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, and Net Banking**

🔐 Your payment is processed through **trusted third-party payment gateways**. We do **not store any card or banking details**.

5. Data Protection & Security

  • 🔒 We store only essential order details.
  • 🚫 We do not track your browsing behavior.
  • 🛡️ Your information is **never sold, rented, or shared**.

6. Cookies & Tracking

🍪 We do **not** use cookies to track personal information. Our website does not collect browsing data.

7. Third-Party Links

🔗 Our website may contain links to other services (e.g., payment gateways). We are **not responsible** for their privacy practices.

8. Changes to Privacy Policy

📢 We may update this policy from time to time. Please check this page regularly for updates.

9. Contact Us

📩 If you have any questions regarding this policy, contact us at: Email: retrocoffeeshop@gmail.com
